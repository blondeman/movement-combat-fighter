The Temple of Spider is the site of ruin for those who believed the old gods would brind the end of times, destroyed in a crusade by those who worshipped them. The temple was generally frequented by Bugs, who were seen as blasphemers and systemically killed. The temple too would fall in time.
In its time the Temple of Spider was a place of peace for those who believed in the Spider queen, Zaire. Zaire was a mother that wove an egg which we know as the sky with the sun being herself checking on her children. The followers thus named themselves the children of Zaire, and were led by eight silksages, know as the mother's legs.
Her followers also believed that deep in the depths of the Crimson Sea, lived another god. Anyone that has fallen into the Sea has never come back up, and it is belived that this god is gathering its strength, to one day devour the world.
