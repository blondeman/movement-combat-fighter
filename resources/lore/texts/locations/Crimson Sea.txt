The seas surrounding the islands of this world has many names, but most call it the Crimson Sea. All who have fallen in have sunk with no sign of survival, and so it has become the focus of many theories and religions. There is general belief that there is life under the sea, and some worship them, while others believe they will the end of the world. Some theories say that there might not be anything, and that it's just not dense enough for anything to swim.
In reality the Crimson Sea is a layer of dense fog, which hides the true nature of these islands. They are actually the backs of giant creatures that feed off particulates in the fog, as well as any poor souls that find their way falling into the Crimson Sea. Being so large means they hardly move, but when they do it causes island wide earthquakes.
