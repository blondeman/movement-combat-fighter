Bugs are a species generally looked down upon by many, and were systemically killed and exiled from the lands inhabitted by other species, who felt they were a lesser being. Only a fraction of their population remain now, in smaller villages and on the outskirts of towns, causing destruction and thieving.
Before they were displaced, Bugs had a very rich culture that can still be seen in other species art forms. Their music, silkwear and visual arts were very influential and other cultures have taken aspects from theirs. Most Bugs worship the Spider queen Zaire.
